About

CoffeeNote for iOS is an app for all coffee lovers. It helps you remenber the favorite cups of coffee and great cafe and moment.

It has various significant attributes according to coffee cupping rule, e.g. origin, quolity of acidity, clean cup, aftertaste...You can log and review your favorite cups of coffee with this app. Let us enjoy great time with coffee.
